🔑 Connect Your SerpAPI Key

PointsMax uses SerpAPI to search live flights. Each user connects their own free key — you get 100 free searches/month and your key is stored only in your browser, never on our servers.

  1. Go to serpapi.com/users/sign_up and create a free account
  2. Copy your API key from the dashboard
  3. Paste it below and click Save

🔒 Your key is saved in your browser's localStorage only. We never store or transmit it to our servers.
